ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Dreyke Boone. I didn't have the smoothest ride getting into both the creative and technical world. Working a dead-end job, I came to the realization that I was wasting my time being unhappy. Taking a leap of faith, I quit my job and drove out to Colorado to work for the summer (or maybe it was to snowboard, still up for debate). After the summer, I returned to Minnesota to pursue a degree in Software Development.

While studying, I connected with a CEO of a startup who brought me onboard. After a couple years, I joined Maximus as a technical product analyst and have been with them ever since.

Currently I’m finishing my degree at Arizona State University and flexing my creative and technical muscles with a high-performing team. My goal is to connect people with brands and businesses that are pushing the boundaries of technology.
